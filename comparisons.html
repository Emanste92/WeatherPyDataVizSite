Comparisons

From the visualizations presented here, we can see that of the four variables, latitude and temperature have the strongest relationship: citites with a latitude closer to 0 are warmer than those farther away in both positive and negative directions. Latitude has weak/negligible relationships between cloudiness and humidity, with significant ceilings at various parts of their respective plots that do not provide clear relationships. A case could be made for a weak relationship between latitude and humidity, with clustering in high levels regardless of latitude, which may hint at a possible relationship influenced by another variable not under consideration in the current dataset.
