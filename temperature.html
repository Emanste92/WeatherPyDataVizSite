Temperature

As expected, cities closer to the equator (with latitude closer to 0) are warmer than those farther away (with latitudes further away from 0 in both positive and negative directions). The tilt of the earth may account for this stronger skewing of warmer temperatures in the southern hemisphere compared to the northern hemisphere around this time of year.
